description: gmeet-unmirror hides the Google Meet presentation tile so full-screen sharing doesn't produce an infinite hall of mirrors.
---

When you share your whole screen on a Google Meet call, Meet's own presentation tile ends up inside the very screen you're sharing — a live feedback loop that recedes into an infinite hall of mirrors. gmeet-unmirror is a small Firefox extension that hides just that tile, on demand or automatically, so screen-sharing stays usable.

## What's here

- [🖱️ Usage](/projects/webext-gmeet-unmirror/docs/usage) — the floating toggle button and the `Alt+Shift+H` shortcut
- [🪞 Auto-hide](/projects/webext-gmeet-unmirror/docs/auto-hide) — automatic detection when a screen share starts, and how manual overrides win
- [🔍 How it works](/projects/webext-gmeet-unmirror/docs/how-it-works) — the detection heuristic and the safety guarantee that keeps everything else on the call untouched
